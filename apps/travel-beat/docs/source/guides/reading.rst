Reading Your Story
==================

Once your story is generated, you can enjoy it in multiple formats.

Story Reader
------------

The built-in story reader provides:

- **Chapter Navigation**: Easy chapter-by-chapter reading
- **Progress Tracking**: Resume where you left off
- **Clean Typography**: Optimized for comfortable reading
- **Responsive Design**: Works on mobile and desktop

Reading Features
----------------

**Table of Contents**
   Jump to any chapter instantly

**Reading Progress**
   Visual indicator of how far you've read

**Chapter Transitions**
   Smooth navigation between chapters

Export Options
--------------

Download your story in different formats:

**Markdown (.md)**
   Plain text with formatting, perfect for:
   
   - Personal archives
   - Blog posts
   - Further editing

**EPUB (Coming Soon)**
   E-book format for:
   
   - Kindle devices
   - Apple Books
   - Other e-readers

**PDF (Coming Soon)**
   Print-ready format for:
   
   - Physical keepsakes
   - Sharing with others

Sharing Your Story
------------------

Share your travel story with friends and family:

1. Navigate to your story
2. Click the "Share" button
3. Copy the shareable link
4. Recipients can read without an account

.. note::
   Shared stories are read-only. Only you can edit or delete.

Story Library
-------------

Access all your stories from the dashboard:

- View all generated stories
- Filter by date or destination
- Search by trip name
- Quick access to continue reading
