Story Generation
================

Travel Beat uses AI to transform your trip itinerary into engaging travel stories.

How It Works
------------

1. **Trip Analysis**: Your destinations, dates, and highlights are analyzed
2. **Chapter Planning**: The AI creates a chapter outline
3. **Content Generation**: Each chapter is written with vivid descriptions
4. **Quality Check**: Content is reviewed for consistency

AI Provider
-----------

Travel Beat uses **Claude** by Anthropic for story generation:

- Claude 3.5 Sonnet for high-quality creative writing
- Context-aware storytelling based on your trip details
- Respectful and culturally sensitive content

Chapter Structure
-----------------

Each generated story includes:

- **Introduction**: Sets the scene for your journey
- **Destination Chapters**: One chapter per major stop
- **Transitions**: Smooth narrative flow between locations
- **Conclusion**: Wraps up your adventure

Customization Options
---------------------

You can influence the story through:

**Protagonist Details**
   Name and gender for personalized narration

**Highlights**
   Specific activities or sights to feature prominently

**Notes**
   Special requests like tone, focus areas, or themes

Generation Time
---------------

Typical generation times:

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Trip Length
     - Chapters
     - Time
   * - Weekend (2-3 days)
     - 3-4
     - ~2 minutes
   * - Week (7 days)
     - 5-7
     - ~3 minutes
   * - Extended (14+ days)
     - 8-12
     - ~5 minutes

Troubleshooting
---------------

**Story stuck on "Generating"**
   Refresh the page. If still stuck, the system will auto-recover.

**Story marked as "Failed"**
   Check your API key configuration. You can retry generation.

**Content not matching expectations**
   Add more detailed highlights and notes for better results.
