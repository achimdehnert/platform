Trip Wizard Guide
=================

The Trip Wizard is a 4-step process to create your personalized travel story.

Step 1: Trip Basics
-------------------

Enter the fundamental details of your trip:

- **Trip Name**: A memorable name for your journey
- **Origin**: Where your adventure begins
- **Start Date**: When your trip starts
- **End Date**: When your trip ends
- **Trip Type**: Solo, Couple, Family, or Group

.. tip::
   Choose a descriptive name that captures the essence of your trip,
   like "Sicilian Summer Adventure" or "Tokyo Food Journey".

Step 2: Destinations & Stops
----------------------------

Add the places you'll visit on your journey:

- **Location**: City or place name
- **Arrival Date**: When you arrive
- **Departure Date**: When you leave
- **Highlights**: What you plan to see or do
- **Notes**: Any special details

You can add multiple stops to create a multi-destination itinerary.

.. note::
   The AI will use your highlights and notes to craft relevant
   story scenes at each location.

Step 3: Story Settings
----------------------

Customize how your story will be generated:

- **Protagonist Name**: The main character (you!)
- **Protagonist Gender**: For proper pronouns
- **User Notes**: Special requests for the AI

The AI will weave your actual trip details into an engaging narrative.

Step 4: Review & Generate
-------------------------

Review all your trip details before generation:

- Verify dates and locations
- Check story settings
- Estimated reading time shown
- Click "Generate Story" to start

Story Generation Process
------------------------

After clicking generate:

1. Your trip is saved to the database
2. A background task starts story generation
3. Progress is shown in real-time
4. Each chapter is generated sequentially
5. You're redirected to read when complete

.. warning::
   Story generation typically takes 2-5 minutes depending on
   trip complexity. Don't close the browser during generation.
